Nicci's favourite movies

The best movies that I have watched!

About Time

This is my favourite romance movie, about a man who discovers the men in his family can travel in time.

Bienvenue chez les Ch'tis

This is one of my favourite French movies, about a man from the trency south of France, who moves to an area in Northern France, called Les Ch'tis. The dialect they speak is very strange and there is a lot of confusion. Very funny IMO.

Pride

I love this LGBTQ+ movie. It's definitely a tearjerker but extremely heartwarming at the same time!
